The schoolog Iivantry was founded in 1627, in order to escape teh tyranical rule of the puritans by use of magic. The orignal sorting hat resembled a capotain hat, however the personality of this magical item became too old fashioned for the school's staff to manage as it spewed horrible demeaning words at the little witches and wizards. Thus, the headmaster of Iivantry at the time, David Crockett, ibued his famous coonskin cap with the ability to sort new students!
